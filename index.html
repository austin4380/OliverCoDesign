About

OliverCoDesign is a self-taught, one-gal shop that strives to create the highest quality handmade macrame pieces. Here at OliverCoDesign, we believe it is important to support other small, independently owned businesses, therefore a goal of ours is to purchase all our supplies from a variety of small businesses, rather than big box stores. Shopping small means supporting somebody's dream and helping to sustain them, and that is at the core of our beliefs. Thank you for shopping small and supporting my business. I hope you love your macrame pieces as much as I love creating them!
